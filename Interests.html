My Interests

European Youth Parliament (EYP)

I spent majority of my spare time doing work for an organisation called the European Youth Parliament. This is a non profit organisation which organises a mock parliament for students aged 15-18. Here they discuss European topics and current affairs in the hope of forming a resolution which is debated in General Assembly. During transition year I partook in these mock parliaments in Cork and Galway. I then became a member of the organisation in 2018 and I have been participating in events around Ireland, Slovenia and Iceland in the past few years more on the media acspect of the organisation. European Affairs and media are massive interests of mine and this organisation combines my two passions which makes it perfect for me.

Scouting & Guiding

I am luckily both a member of Scouting Ireland and The Irish Girl Guides. I am a leader with the Brownie unit locally and I am a member of a scout group in West Cork. In scouts we do activites such as pioneering, orienteering and hiking. I enjoy hiking the most as its a chance to travel around Ireland and see the stunning landscape from the peak of mountains. I also enjoy the practical side of pioneering. Over the summer with my scout group we completed the last leg of the camino. We travelled to Spain and walked 120km which was the most rewarding expierence of my life. In guiding, I was a ladybird, brownie and guide which means I have been a member since 2005. Guiding is more learning skills such as first aid, crafts and camping. I love volunteering with other leaders as it is so rewarding working with younder children.

Travel

To all who know me, its so surprise I have the travel bug. When I was a little girl my parents taught me the importance of travel so for the first 10 years of my life we travelled to Paris, London and around Ireland. Then in my early teens it kicked up a gear as my parents bought a caravan. My family and my grandparents travelled around the continent hitting France, Belgium, Luxembourg, Germany, The Netherlands, Switzerland, Italy, England and Wales, Poland and Austria. We travelled around the capitals and small towns of these countries, the list would be to long to type and I'm very lucky for this. As I got older and involved in the European Youth Parliament I got to trael independently with the organisation going to Slovenia in 2018 and Iceland in 2019. I also will soon be travelling to Albania in 4 weeks for a EYP session there. I am eager to travel and my family support me in that. My family and I are travelling to America in June also.
